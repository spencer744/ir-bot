## Current Property Condition

Parkview Commons was built in 2001 and is in good structural condition. The property features wood-frame construction with brick and Hardy-plank exteriors, pitched composition roofs, and individual HVAC systems in each unit. Building systems including plumbing, electrical, and water heaters are in serviceable condition with remaining useful life that extends well beyond the five-year hold period.

Unit interiors are largely in their original 2001 configuration and represent the primary value-add opportunity. Current finishes include laminate countertops, builder-grade white appliances, carpet flooring, basic brass fixtures, and dated lighting packages. A small number of units have received partial cosmetic updates from the current ownership, but no comprehensive renovation program has been undertaken.
