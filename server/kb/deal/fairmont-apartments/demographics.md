## Notes & Cross-References

### † 1-Mile Population Growth
Both tracts within 1 mile (007214 and 007215) did not exist in the 2019 ACS 5-year estimates — they were carved out of existing tracts to capture the new Westerville/New Albany development boom. No statistically valid 5-year comparison is possible at the 1-mile radius. The 3-mile growth of +5.6% on comparable tracts is the best proxy.

### ‡ Rent-to-Own Cost Ratio
Rent-to-own ratio calculated as median rent / estimated monthly cost of homeownership. Monthly ownership cost estimate based on median home value with 20% down, 30-year mortgage at 7.0%, plus property taxes (~1.6% Franklin County effective rate) and insurance. The investor deck (citing ESRI/Newmark) states the market average is a **63% monthly discount** to homeownership — avg homeownership cost ~$4,831/mo vs. Fairmont avg rent ~$1,836/mo.
