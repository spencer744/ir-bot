# Fairmont Apartments — Deal Overview

## The Opportunity

Gray Capital is acquiring Fairmont Apartments, a 219-unit, 2025-vintage Class A multifamily community in Westerville, Ohio — on the west edge of New Albany, Columbus' most supply-constrained and affluent submarket. This is a brand-new, fully stabilized asset with a 15-year tax abatement, zero capex required, and ~$290K of embedded loss-to-lease poised to capture as leases roll to market.

We are entering as the supply cycle reverses. New deliveries are falling sharply, Anduril Arsenal-1 begins production (4,008 jobs) three months post-close, and Intel Ohio One (~3,000 engineers) arrives during our hold period. We're not betting on market recovery — we're stepping into it.

## Why Fairmont

**1. Buy below replacement cost with a structural cash flow advantage.**
At $285K/door, we're acquiring a 2025-vintage asset at an estimated 15–20% discount to replacement cost ($350K+/door). Fairmont also carries a 15-year, 100% tax abatement on improved value through 2041 — generating ~$895K in Year 1 tax savings that no comparable asset can replicate. Adjusting for the abatement's $12.2M NPV, the effective basis drops to ~$230K/door, in-line with older, less-amenitized product.

**2. Nearly 6% loss-to-lease sitting in the rent roll.**
The developer underpriced leases during the peak supply cycle to fill the building. ~$290K/year of embedded loss-to-lease is captured on natural lease rollover — no renovation required. 46% of leases roll by August 2026. 3BR units are currently 5–8% below submarket; we'll push 5–10% on renewal.

**3. Supply cliff + demand catalyst timing.**
Westerville new deliveries drop 75% in 2026. Anduril Arsenal-1 (4,008 jobs, $132K avg salary) begins production July 2026 — three months after close. Intel Ohio One (~3,000 engineers at $135K+) arrives 2030–31, compressing exit cap rates precisely when we go to market. These are free call options layered on top of our base case.

**4. Best-in-market amenity package, no CapEx required.**
Two heated pools, TrackMan golf simulator, dual Starbucks coffee bars, private screening room, co-working spaces, 24-hour fitness. This is the asset others will be compared to. Zero capital spend required during the hold period.

**5. Agency debt locked in, IO period extended.**
$43.75M Fannie Mae loan via Greystone at 5.17% fixed, with 48 months of interest-only. Gray Capital self-manages, capturing the management margin directly and maintaining full operational control.

## The Exit

Underwritten exit at 5.50% cap rate in June 2033. At exit, ~8 years of abatement remain — representing an estimated $7–8M NPV to the next buyer. That's a structural pricing floor no unabated comparable carries. At 6.00% exit cap (50 bps of widening), the deal still clears 12%+ IRR.

**Exit cap sensitivity:**
- 5.25% (Bull): ~$86M+ sale price (~$393K+/door)
- 5.50% (Base): ~$82.7M (~$378K/door)
- 5.75% (Bear): ~$79.2M (~$362K/door)
