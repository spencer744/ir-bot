# FAQ: Investment Process, Steps & Timeline

**Q: What are the steps to invest in a Gray Capital deal?**

A: The investment process follows these steps:

### Step 1: Review the Opportunity
Access the Deal Room for the investment summary, financial projections, market analysis, business plan, property photos, and webinar recording. Fairmont Apartments materials include the full underwriting model (Conservative, Base, and Upside scenarios), property condition reports, market data, and the business plan detail.

### Step 2: Schedule a Call (Optional)
Schedule a one-on-one call with Blake Pieroni (Blake@graycapitalllc.com) or Griffin Haddad (Griffin@graycapitalllc.com) to discuss the deal, underwriting, fees, and how it fits your portfolio. No obligation.

### Step 3: Verify Accredited Investor Status
This offering is for accredited investors only under SEC Regulation D, Rule 506(c). Verification methods: recent tax returns/W-2s showing income above $200,000 individual or $300,000 joint, a letter from a CPA/attorney/RIA, or statements confirming net worth exceeding $1 million (excluding primary residence). Gray Capital uses a third-party verification service.

### Step 4: Complete Subscription Documents
Complete documents through the secure investor portal:
- **Subscription Agreement** -- Investment commitment and terms acknowledgment
- **Operating Agreement** -- Governing document defining rights and economic terms
- **PPM** -- Full disclosure document with projections, risk factors, and legal terms
- **Investor Questionnaire** -- Suitability and accreditation verification
- **W-9 (or W-8BEN)** -- Tax identification for K-1 preparation

All documents available for e-signature via DocuSign. Minimum investment: $100,000.

### Step 5: Fund Your Investment
After subscription acceptance, wire funds within five business days. Accepted methods:
- **Wire Transfer** (preferred, fastest)
- **ACH Transfer** (additional processing time)
- **Self-Directed IRA or Solo 401(k)** -- Custodian wires on your behalf; allow 5-10 extra business days.

### Step 6: Receive Confirmation
Once funds are verified, you receive a countersigned subscription agreement and portal access credentials. The portal provides 24/7 access to documents, distributions, reports, and tax documents.

### Step 7: Property Closing
The acquisition closes on schedule. You receive a closing confirmation and are officially a limited partner in Fairmont Apartments.

**Q: How long does the entire process take?**

A: Typically one to two weeks from initial interest to funded investment:

| Milestone | Timeline |
|-----------|----------|
| Review Deal Room materials | Days 1-3 |
| IR call (optional) | Days 2-5 |
| Accredited investor verification | Days 3-7 |
| Sign subscription documents | Days 5-10 |
| Wire funds | Days 7-14 |
| Confirmation and portal access | Within 2 business days of funding |

Timeline varies based on entity/retirement account structures which may require additional documentation. Popular offerings can fill before deadlines -- begin early to secure your allocation.

**Q: When will I receive my first distribution after investing?**

A: Typically within 60-90 days after property closing. The initial period allows for property transition, account setup, and business plan execution. After the first distribution, payments are monthly via ACH.

**Q: What happens after I invest? What ongoing communication should I expect?**

A: Gray Capital provides:
- **Monthly Distributions** -- Direct deposit with statements in the portal
- **Monthly Operational Updates** -- Occupancy, leasing, and rent growth highlights
- **Quarterly Financial Reports** -- Financials, performance vs. underwriting, market commentary, updated projections
- **Annual K-1 Tax Documents** -- Delivered by March 15 via the portal
- **Investor Portal** -- 24/7 access to all documents and history
- **Ad Hoc Communications** -- Material updates as they occur

**Q: What is the investor portal?**

A: A secure online platform with 24/7 access to subscription documents, distribution statements, quarterly reports, K-1s, property photos, renovation updates, and your personal investment summary showing estimated value, total distributions, and projected returns.

**Q: Can I invest in multiple Gray Capital deals?**

A: Yes. Each deal is a separate LLC/LP, so investments are independent. No limit on the number of offerings, subject to $100,000 minimum per deal. Existing LPs receive priority access to new offerings.

**Q: What if I have questions during or after the investment process?**

A: Contact the IR team anytime:
- **Email:** Blake Pieroni (Blake@graycapitalllc.com) or Griffin Haddad (Griffin@graycapitalllc.com)
- **Phone/Video:** Schedule directly with Blake or Griffin

Response time target: one business day.

**Q: What if the deal fills up before I complete my subscription?**

A: Fairmont Apartments equity raise is $15 million. Subscriptions are first-come, first-served, and popular offerings can fill early. Existing LPs receive priority access. If fully subscribed, you are placed on a waitlist and receive priority notification of the next offering.
