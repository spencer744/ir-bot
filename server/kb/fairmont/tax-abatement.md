## Why This Matters

Real estate taxes are one of the largest operating expenses for multifamily properties. A 15-year tax abatement significantly:

1. **Boosts NOI** — Lower tax expense = higher net operating income = higher cash flow to investors
2. **Supports distributions** — More NOI means more distributable cash during the hold
3. **Increases value at exit** — Higher NOI → lower cap rate denominator → higher valuation multiple
4. **Enhances IRR** — The abatement benefit compounds across the full hold period

**Critically: We are in Year 1 of 15.** This means the full 15-year benefit runs through approximately 2041. For a 5–7 year hold, the abatement covers the ENTIRE projected hold period.
