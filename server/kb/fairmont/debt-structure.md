## Why Agency Debt?

**Fannie Mae multifamily loans are best-in-class for several reasons:**

1. **Fixed rate** — 4.97% is locked for the entire 10-year term. No refinancing risk, no floating rate exposure, no interest rate cap required.

2. **48-month IO period** — During the renovation and lease-up phase (years 1–4), debt payments are interest-only. This maximizes cash flow precisely when it's most needed and before stabilization is achieved.

3. **Non-recourse** — The loan is non-recourse to investors. In a worst-case scenario, investor liability is limited to invested equity.

4. **10-year term** — Provides optionality. We can exit via sale at year 5–7 or refinance at year 10 if market conditions favor a hold.

---

## Interest-Only Period — What It Means for Investors

During months 1–48, the monthly debt payment is interest-only. This means:

- **Lower debt service** during renovation and stabilization
- **Higher NOI** relative to fully amortizing loan
- **Better early cash-on-cash** potential during years 1–4
- **No principal paydown** during IO — loan balance remains at $41.275M through month 48
