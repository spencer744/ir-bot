# FAQ – General Investment Questions

---

### Who is eligible to invest in Fairmont at Westerville Commons?

- The offering is open to accredited investors only, as defined by SEC Regulation D, Rule 501. Additional requirements may be set forth in the PPM.

---

### What is the minimum investment?

- $100,000, unless otherwise stated in supplemental offering documents.

---

### How do I indicate interest or request more information?

- Use the “Indicate Interest” button in the deal room or contact Griffin Haddad directly at ir@graycapitalllc.com. A formal commitment is not required until you decide to proceed.

---

### How do I subscribe?

- The full process: indicate interest, review the PPM, complete the subscription agreement, provide any required documentation, and fund your investment. Details are in the Documents section and will be provided by the Investor Relations team upon request.

---

### Are distributions guaranteed?

- No. All projected distributions are estimates based on current assumptions. See the PPM for detailed risk disclosure.

---
