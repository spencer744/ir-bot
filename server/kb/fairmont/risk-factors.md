# Risk Factors — Fairmont at Westerville Commons

---

This section outlines key risks associated with this investment. These are intended to supplement, not replace, the full disclosure of risk factors in the Private Placement Memorandum (PPM).

## Key Risks

1. **Illiquidity Risk**  
   - There is no secondary market for interests in this offering. Investors should be prepared to hold their investment for the full term (projected 5–7 years).
2. **Market/Economic Risk**  
   - Adverse changes in the economy or Columbus real estate market could negatively impact occupancy, rents, or exit cap rates.
3. **Interest Rate Risk**  
   - While the debt is fixed, future refinancing or market exit values could be affected by macro rate moves.
4. **Execution/Renovation Risk**  
   - Delays or overruns in the renovation schedule could impact projected returns. The property management team will directly oversee project execution.
5. **Concentration Risk**  
   - This is a single-asset investment. Results depend on the performance of this individual property.
6. **Regulatory/Tax Abatement Risk**  
   - Loss of the tax abatement would materially reduce NOI and cash flow. See "Tax Abatement" KB file for details.
7. **Sponsor Risk**  
   - Investment returns depend on the skill of Gray Capital and its affiliates.

---

**Mitigations:**
- Conservative underwriting stress-tests negative scenarios (see financial summary)
- 15-year tax abatement secured at close
- Fixed-rate agency debt, long IO period for cushion
- Experienced in-house management (Gray Residential)
- $800,000+ operating reserves at acquisition
