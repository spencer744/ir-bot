# FAQ — Returns, Distributions & Waterfall

---

### What are the return targets for this deal?
- Target IRR (Base): 13.2%
- Target Equity Multiple: ~1.85x
- Target Cash-on-Cash (stabilized): 5.5%

---

### Are projected returns guaranteed?
No. All returns are estimates based on current assumptions. They are not guaranteed and actual results may differ materially.

---

### When are distributions paid?
- Distributions are targeted quarterly beginning [after stabilization]. Return of capital and profits are distributed at exit (via refinance or sale).

---

### What is the preferred return and promote/GP split?
- Preferred return: 8% annual, cumulative, not compounding
- Above 8% hurdle: 70% LP / 30% GP
- Above 15% hurdle: 65% LP / 35% GP
- No GP catch-up structure

---

### Can I reinvest distributions?
No automatic reinvestment. All distributions are paid out and must be manually reinvested in new deals if desired.

---

### What is the estimated tax treatment of returns?
- Investors receive a K-1 annually. Significant year 1 loss (estimated 30% of invested amount) is projected due to cost segregation/bonus depreciation (subject to final tax study/results). Consult your tax advisor for your specific situation.
