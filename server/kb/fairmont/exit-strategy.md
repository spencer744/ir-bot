# Exit Strategy — Fairmont at Westerville Commons

---

## Planned Hold & Exit
- Target hold: 5–7 years (modeled at 6 years in base case)
- Typical exit scenarios:
  1. Sale to institutional or private buyer at market cap rate
  2. Refinance into new agency/bridge loan (if market conditions/valuation are favorable)

---

## Sale Scenario (Primary)
- Underwritten to 5.25% exit cap in base case
- Value maximized by rent-to-market capture, improved amenities, full occupancy
- Brokered exit with regional/national multifamily investment sales specialists

---

## Refinance Scenario (Alternative)
- Only pursued if value creation and debt markets warrant a cash-out refi
- 10-year Fannie loan provides optionality: can refinance within term or at maturity
