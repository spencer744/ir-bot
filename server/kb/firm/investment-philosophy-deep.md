# Gray Capital -- Investment Philosophy

## Core Thesis

Gray Capital's conviction: multifamily real estate in Midwest markets offers the best risk-adjusted returns in commercial real estate. Backed by $2B+ transaction volume, 7,500+ units, and 29.3% average IRR across 10 full-cycle deals with zero capital losses.

Three pillars:
1. **Multifamily is the most resilient CRE asset class.** Necessity-based demand persists through cycles. Monthly lease rollovers hedge inflation. Significant tax advantages via depreciation. Ability to force appreciation through operational improvements.
2. **Midwest offers superior risk-adjusted returns.** Lower basis, stronger CoC yields, favorable rent-to-income ratios, less institutional competition. Increasingly diversified employment (healthcare, education, logistics, tech, manufacturing).
3. **Vertical integration ensures consistent execution.** Controlling property management (Gray Residential), construction (Gray Construction & Design), and capital management (Gray Capital) eliminates misaligned incentives and execution risk.

## Deal Evaluation Framework

### Stage 1: Market Screening
Evaluates population growth, employment diversity, income growth, supply pipeline, rent-to-income ratios. Targets markets with positive fundamentals where institutional capital hasn't compressed cap rates. Current markets: Indianapolis, Evansville, Bloomington, Muncie, Carmel, Noblesville, plus expansion in Dayton (OH), Champaign (IL), Des Moines (IA), Okemos (MI).

### Stage 2: Property-Level Analysis
Location quality, physical condition, unit mix, operations, competitive positioning, value-add potential. In-person visits and unit-by-unit inspections. Key metrics: current NOI, in-place vs. market rents, occupancy trends, deferred maintenance, CapEx requirements.

### Stage 3: Four-Scenario Underwriting

| Scenario | Rent Growth | Vacancy | Expense Growth | Exit Cap Rate | Reno Costs | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 0-1% | +2-3% above market | 3-4% | +50-75bps | +10-15% | +6-12 months |
| Base | 2-3% | At market | 2-3% | Flat to entry | On budget | On schedule |
| Aggressive | 4-5% | Below market | 1-2% | -25-50bps | On/below budget | Ahead |
| Home Run | Exceptional tailwinds | -- | -- | Significant compression | -- | -- |

Every deal must generate acceptable returns at the Conservative level.

### Stage 4: Investment Committee
Led by CIO Jay Reeder and CEO Spencer Gray. Evaluates risk factors, market dynamics, operational complexity, capital requirements, and portfolio fit.

## Risk Management

- **Leverage:** 65-75% LTV, prefer fixed-rate or rate-capped debt. Avoid unhedged floating-rate exposure.
- **Reserves:** Adequate operating, CapEx, and interest rate reserves. Never stripped to boost projected returns. Portfolio performed through COVID without capital calls or distribution suspensions.
- **Diversification:** Geographic concentration in Midwest for operational advantages, but diversified across submarkets, vintage, unit count, and strategy. Portfolio spans IN, OH, IL, IA, MI.
- **Insurance:** Comprehensive property/liability, Phase I environmental assessments, umbrella coverage.
- **Operational Control:** Vertical integration reduces execution risk and enables rapid response to challenges.

## Value Creation Playbook

### Revenue Enhancement
- **Unit Renovations:** Interior upgrades commanding $150-$300+ monthly premiums (executed by Gray Construction & Design)
- **Amenity Upgrades:** Fitness centers, dog parks, outdoor spaces, package lockers
- **Revenue Management:** Dynamic pricing, lease expiration management, ancillary income (pet fees, parking, storage, RUBS)
- **Occupancy Optimization:** Aggressive leasing, online presence, reputation management, retention programs

### Expense Optimization
- Property tax appeals, portfolio-level insurance procurement, utility efficiency (low-flow fixtures, LEDs, HVAC upgrades), vendor consolidation across 7,500+ units

### Capital Structure
- Refinancing to return capital, reduce debt costs, or extend maturities when favorable
- Supplemental loans on agency-financed properties after NOI growth

## Deal Structure & Alignment
- **Minimum Investment:** $100,000
- **Preferred Return:** 8% cumulative to LPs
- **Profit Split:** 70/30 (LP/GP) above preferred
- **Sponsor Co-Investment:** 14% average
- **Distributions:** Monthly after stabilization
- **Fees:** Acquisition (1.75%), Loan Guarantee (0.75%), Asset Management (0.50%), Property Management (3.0%), Construction Management (5.0%)
