# Gray Capital -- The Investor Experience

## Overview

Gray Capital provides transparency, accessibility, and confidence at every stage: inquiry, investment, ownership, distributions, and exit. Investor relations is led by Blake Pieroni (Head of IR) and Griffin Haddad (Investment Associate).

## The Investment Process

### Step 1: Introduction and Education
Contact via email (Blake@graycapitalllc.com, Griffin@graycapitalllc.com), phone (317-203-9457), or website. Resources include:
- **The Gray Report:** Spencer Gray's multifamily intelligence newsletter
- **Track Record:** All 10 realized deals published (29.3% avg IRR, 2.16x avg equity multiple, zero capital losses)
- **Video Content:** Regular market/portfolio updates from Spencer Gray
- **Investment Guides:** Syndication structures, tax benefits, deal mechanics

No pressure to invest; thorough due diligence encouraged.

### Step 2: Deal Presentation
Each opportunity includes: investment summary, detailed offering memorandum, four-scenario underwriting (Conservative/Base/Aggressive/Home Run), market analysis, business plan, and complete deal structure/fee disclosure.

### Step 3: Commitment and Closing
1. Sign subscription documents (LP agreement, accredited investor verification)
2. Wire investment ($100,000 minimum)
3. Confirmation and investor portal access
4. Welcome package with investment summary, contacts, distribution schedule, report access

### Step 4: Ongoing Communication
- **Quarterly Reports:** Property financials, occupancy, renovation progress, CapEx updates, market conditions, narrative commentary
- **Monthly Distribution Statements:** Amount, period, running total
- **Annual K-1s:** Distributed in Q1; IR team available for tax questions
- **Video Updates:** Regular CEO updates on portfolio and market
- **Ad Hoc Updates:** Timely communication for milestones, refinancings, strategy changes

## Distributions

- **Preferred Return:** 8% cumulative before sponsor participates in profits
- **Profit Split:** 70/30 (LP/GP) above preferred return
- **Frequency:** Monthly (not quarterly like many syndications)
- **Method:** ACH direct deposit or check

Distributions begin after initial stabilization. Value-add: may be several months post-closing. Core-plus (e.g., Villas on Fir targeting 15% CoC): may begin shortly after closing.
