# Gray Capital -- Leadership Team

Gray Capital's leadership spans real estate investment, construction, property operations, investor relations, and marketing. The vertically integrated model requires deep competency across the full multifamily investment lifecycle.

---

## Alex Gray -- Co-Founder & President, Gray Construction & Design

Leads all construction operations: unit renovations, capital improvements, and development initiatives. Gray Construction & Design is a core component of vertical integration -- controlling construction in-house eliminates margin and timeline risk from third-party contractors.

Alex's team manages every renovation from scoping and budgeting through procurement, execution, and quality control. Has completed thousands of unit renovations (kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, lighting, fixtures) and exterior improvements (roofing, siding, parking, amenities). Works closely with Spencer Gray and the investment team during underwriting to develop accurate renovation scopes and budgets grounded in real-world experience.

---

## Jay Reeder -- Chief Investment Officer

Oversees investment strategy, deal sourcing, underwriting, and capital markets. Leads Gray Capital's proprietary four-scenario underwriting model (Conservative, Base, Aggressive, Home Run) for every prospective deal.

Responsibilities: ensuring return projections use conservative assumptions, overseeing asset management across 17 active projects, monitoring property performance against underwriting, managing debt capital provider relationships, and securing favorable financing terms. His analytical discipline has been instrumental in building the zero-capital-loss track record across 10 realized deals.

---

## Andrew Bosway -- Chief Operating Officer

Responsible for day-to-day operations, organizational infrastructure, and cross-functional coordination. Oversees financial reporting, technology infrastructure, compliance, HR, and vendor relationships across 17 active projects in multiple states.

Ensures the vertically integrated model functions smoothly by facilitating communication between investment, property management, and construction teams. Contributes to strategic planning with Spencer Gray on organizational priorities, resource allocation, and growth.

---

## Katrina Greene -- Director of Property Management

Leads all property management operations for Gray Residential: leasing, revenue management, resident relations, and site-level staff management. Manages regional managers, community managers, leasing consultants, and admin staff.

Sets/monitors occupancy targets, implements dynamic pricing, manages renewals, oversees retention programs. Under her leadership, Gray Residential consistently outperforms third-party alternatives. Works with the investment team during underwriting to validate operational assumptions and leasing projections. Also oversees resident experience initiatives (move-in process, maintenance systems, community events, online reputation).

---

## Joe Powell -- Director of Facilities

Oversees physical plant maintenance, CapEx planning, and facilities operations across the full portfolio. Manages maintenance teams, preventive maintenance programs, and vendor relationships.

Covers HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, parking, landscaping, common areas, and unit maintenance. Tracks work order response times, completion rates, and satisfaction scores. Coordinates with Alex Gray's construction team during value-add renovations to maintain service quality for existing residents. Assesses physical condition of prospective acquisitions and provides input on deferred maintenance budgets and staffing.

Manages capital raising, coordinates closings and subscription documents, and ensures every investor understands their investment before committing. Committed to transparency and prompt responses.
